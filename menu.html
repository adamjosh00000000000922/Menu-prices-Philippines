Menu Prices in the Philippines

Introduction

Eating out in the Philippines offers a diverse culinary experience, from street food to fine dining. Understanding menu prices can help both locals and tourists budget their dining experiences more effectively. This article provides a comprehensive overview of menu prices across various types of eateries in the Philippines. To read more about it visit menusprice

Street Food

  • Isaw (Grilled Chicken Intestines): PHP 10-15 per stick
  • Balut (Fertilized Duck Egg): PHP 15-20 per piece
  • Kwek-Kwek (Quail Eggs in Orange Batter): PHP 10-15 per piece
